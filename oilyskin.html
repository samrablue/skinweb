Moisturizing is a very important step if you have oily skin. “There’s some belief that if you have oily skin, you don’t need to or shouldn’t moisturize; All skin types need moisturizer, but if you have oily skin, you should be more careful with what kind of moisturizer you’re using” Look for a moisturizer that’s:
  • lightweight
  • free of oils
  • water-based
